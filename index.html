Proof of Value

Here are a few examples of how I've put my approach into action, delivering tangible results and driving transformation.

University-Wide Cloud Modernization

Situation: At Dallas Baptist University, we faced aging on-premise infrastructure that was costly and couldn't scale to meet our future needs. Leadership was interested in the cloud but needed a clear business case to justify the investment.

Task: My role was to translate the technical benefits of Azure into a strategic and financial plan, aligning our approach with the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework to ensure a well-governed and successful migration.

Action: I led discovery workshops across departments to map our application portfolio. I then built a comprehensive business case with detailed TCO/ROI analysis and a multi-year roadmap, which I presented to senior leadership.

Result: The plan was approved, leading to a 300% increase in Azure consumption and a simultaneous reduction in our Total Cost of Ownership. We successfully modernized our core infrastructure, increasing agility and security across the university.

Identity and Access Management - Modernization

Situation: The process for creating and managing user accounts was manual, relying on a series of disconnected scripts. This was inefficient, prone to error, and created security risks.

Task: My objective was to orchestrate the development and delivery of a modern, automated solution that would streamline the entire user account lifecycle.

Action: As the project leader, I orchestrated a team to design and build a new system using modern web applications in Azure with Python and FastAPI. I managed the project from conception to deployment, ensuring we removed roadblocks and delivered a secure, reliable solution.

Result: We successfully replaced the legacy scripts with a robust, cloud-native application. This dramatically improved operational efficiency, reduced manual errors, and enhanced our security posture by standardizing the account management process.

Driving the Adoption of Generative AI

Situation: With the rapid advancements in Generative AI, the university's leadership recognized a critical opportunity to enhance both student learning and administrative efficiency.

Task: My goal is to act as a catalyst for growth by developing a strategic vision for AI adoption, aligning our initiatives with the Microsoft AI Adoption Framework to ensure we invest in the right areas for maximum impact.

Action: I am guiding the strategy by evaluating platforms like Azure OpenAI and identifying high-value use cases across the university. I am building the business cases needed to secure stakeholder buy-in and champion these new initiatives.

Result: This forward-looking strategy positions the university to be a leader in educational technology. We are creating a roadmap to unlock new levels of innovation and efficiency, ensuring we are prepared for the next wave of technological transformation.
